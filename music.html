Music Work

Fight For Asran

My newest piece for DigiPen's Jazz Dragons arranged throughout late 2024 and into early 2025. First debuted by the Jazz Dragons in late April of the same year. Originally called Oil Field Base and done as an arrangement of the level of the same name, this piece turned into a suite with custom transitions to showcase some of my favorite songs and moments from the SNES classic U.N. Squadron. All music transcribed and arranged was composed by Manami Matsumae and helps to convey the journey as you fight for the mighty country of Asran.

Driftveil City

This piece was arranged in June of 2024 and brought to DigiPen's Jazz Dragons in September of the same year, where we performed it for the first time that December. Driftveil City comes from Pokemon Black and White and was composed by Hitomi Sato featuring an iconic melody, deep groovy bassline, and some perfect jazzy chords for a 12-piece jazz ensemble.
